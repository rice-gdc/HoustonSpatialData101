## ArcGIS Online 
To access the full range of spatial datasets in ArcGIS, log into the [Rice ArcGIS Organization](https://riceuniversity.atlassian.net/wiki/external/ZjNkMDZkNzcxYzVhNGVjZWE5NzMyZDgyYmM2NGQxMTM). 

### ArcGIS Living Atlas
[ArcGIS Living Atlas of the World](https://livingatlas.arcgis.com/en/home/) is trusted, authortative collection of spatial data managed by the developers of ArcGIS, Esri. Living Atlas datasets tend to be national or global in nature, but since Houston is a part of the United States and the world, there's a lot of useful data here. Datasets can be browsed by type or theme and searched by keyword. **Click** the *waffle* in the upper right of the [ArcGIS Online home page](https://ricegis.maps.arcgis.com/) and select [Living Atlas](https://livingatlas.arcgis.com/en/home/) to begin browsing or searching. 

### ArcGIS Online 
In addition to the Living Atlas, many other spatial datasets from government agencies, universities, and researchers to casual users are uploaded and stored in the ArcGIS Online system. Since any user can upload data, there's no guarentee that this data is up-to-date, accurate or high-quality. Search by Rice University or the entire world using the large search bar at the top of the ArcGIS Online homepage.
